Game Rules

  • 1. Select a chip to add to the bet; all 4 circles will get that chip (same total).
  • 2. Dealer gets 5 cards, Player gets 3 cards.
  • 3. Compare player's 3-card to dealer's partial or best 3-card as the dealer's cards are revealed (2-card, 3-card, 4-card, 5-card).
  • 4. If dealer wins at any stage, player loses all bets.
  • 5. Player can stop at any winning stage for partial payouts or continue for bigger potential payouts.

Payouts

  • Beating a 2-card dealer hand:
    • (2) pays 1:1, (3)/(4)/(5) push
  • Beating a 3-card dealer hand:
    • (2)/(3) pay 1:1, (4)/(5) push
  • Beating a 4-card dealer hand:
    • (2)/(3) pay 1:1, (4) pays 2:1, (5) push
  • Beating a 5-card dealer hand:
    • (2)/(3) pay 1:1, (4) pays 2:1, (5) pays 4:1
